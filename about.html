About us

Principal Investigator

Dr. Srikant Srinivasan

School of Computing and Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology, Mandi

Résumé

Ph.D. , ECE, Purdue University - West Lafayette, IN (2012)
M.S. , ECE, University of Cincinnati, Cincinnati, OH (2005)
B.Tech. , ECE, Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad, India, (2003)

Welcome to my research webpage! I am a researcher with a diverse background rooted in Electrical Engineering. My primary training was in the area of nanoelectronics during the course of my PhD degree program. Since then I spent about 5 years developing and applying my basic electrical engineering knowledge to the areas of sensor networks, cyber physical systems, big data, machine learning, image processing and high dimensional data analysis. I presently serve as an Assistant Professor at IIT - Mandi, India where I am exploring the research areas below.
